The Best Movies according to Daddy

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Thing (1982)

This is my favorite horror movie.
Now, I will point out that I'm not a huge horror movie fan, but this movie hit a little differently.
It was creative and the monster was much more ominous and almost like something written by H.P. Lovecraft.
It was creative and made me interested in a lot of themes, movies, and shows that became all-time favorites for me.

The Hunchback of Notre Dame

This is by far one of my favorite Disney movies.
Growing up I would watch it all the time and I'll never forget how deep and complex the story arc and characters are.
Something about the movie just kept with me growing up; from the humor to the dark, gruesome reality of the story (that I wouldn't understand until I was older).

Hotel Transylvania (all the parts)

This is my favourite cartoon.
Also a beautiful genre.
